Our Vision

dvctvs conducts research in socio-technical and distributed systems with focus on digital identity. Whenever technologies scrutinise natural persons, developers need to take a stance. We provide the expertise and managerial capacity to lead projects, where attestation services are built.

We keep emerging technologies in check.

Our Mission

dvctvs leads project management for decentralised identity projects that are building on distributed technology stacks, including W3C verifiable credentials, OpenID Connect for verifiable presentation and credential issuance, ERC-721 and ERC-6551, as well as PKI-based identity systems.

We strive for excellence.
